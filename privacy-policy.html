Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver and improve our service:

  • Uploaded Documents: The PDF or image files you explicitly upload to eApply for form filling. These may contain personal data (e.g., name, address, ID numbers). eApply extracts relevant fields from these documents to autofill forms.
  • Extracted Data: The form data parsed from your documents. We securely store this data (with encryption) so you can reuse it for future form submissions with a single click.
  • Account and Billing Information: If you register for an account or subscribe to a paid plan, we collect identifiers like your email address and payment details (processed via a secure third-party gateway).
  • Usage Data: Technical and usage data about your interactions with eApply (e.g., browser version, number of forms filled, feature usage). This helps us optimize performance and fix issues.
  • Cookies and Local Storage: The extension may use cookies or browser local storage to save your preferences or session (e.g., keeping you logged in). These are used only for core functionality such as authentication and settings.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the eApply service:

  • Form Automation: We process uploaded documents and extracted data to automatically fill forms on compatible websites. This is the primary purpose of data collection.
  • Service Improvement: We analyze usage patterns and technical data to enhance features and performance. (For example, some services use data solely to power AI-based form filling and improve accuracy.)
  • Communication: If you provide contact details, we may send service-related notices such as account updates, support responses, and feature announcements.
  • Legal Compliance & Security: We use data to comply with applicable laws and to detect, prevent, or respond to fraud, abuse, and security incidents.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We respect your privacy and will never sell or rent your personal information. We only share data in the limited circumstances described below:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party vendors who perform services for eApply, such as hosting, data processing, printing, or analytics. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and may only use it to deliver services on our behalf.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, regulation, or legal process, such as a subpoena or court order, or when requested by government or law enforcement authorities.
  • Business Transfers: If eApply is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to the protections of this Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our service and comply with legal obligations. For example, uploaded documents and extracted data are kept while your account is active and may be deleted when you remove them or after a period of inactivity.

Billing and security-related records may be retained for longer periods when required for tax, legal, or fraud prevention purposes.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • European Users (GDPR): You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You may also withdraw consent at any time and, in some cases, request data portability or lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.
  • California Users (CCPA/CPRA): You may request disclosure of the personal information collected about you and how it is used. You also have the right to request deletion of your data and opt out of any sale of personal information (we do not sell personal data), and you will not be discriminated against for exercising your rights.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

eApply does not use third-party tracking cookies. We use only essential cookies and browser storage APIs (such as Chrome storage) to support core functionality, including authentication and user preferences.

Disabling essential storage or cookies may impact the proper functioning of the service.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure hosting, to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection against unauthorized access.

In the event of a security incident, we will take appropriate steps to mitigate the impact and notify you where required by law.

Children's Privacy

eApply is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that such data has been collected, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with a revised “Last Updated” date. Continued use of the service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
